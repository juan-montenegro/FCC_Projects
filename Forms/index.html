Nikola Tesla

One of the greatest inventors in human history

Short biography:

  • Born in 10 July 1856 Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia).

  • Inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, physicist, and a futurist. Best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system

"Let the future tell the truth, and evaluate each one according to his work and accomplishments. The present is theirs; the future, for which I have really worked, is mine." -- Nikola Tesla
